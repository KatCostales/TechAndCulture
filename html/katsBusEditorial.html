Impakter

Impakter was founded in Rome in 2014 and established “a global platform that empowers sustainability through news, articles, startups, and products.” The Impakter platform centers its stories and experiences around sustainability. The company objectives aim to research and evaluate global companies and assess their sustainability efforts. Impakter is a business that aids companies begin their eco-friendly journey by evaluating their actions and generating clear, concise actions plans and reports.
Impakter’s mission statement “aims to raise collective moral awareness through the lens of IMPAKTER through encouraged dialogues between Millennials and key decision makers.” The credibility of Impakter articles is strengthened by expert writers/leaders/ thinkers who are associated with the United Nations, NGOs, Institutes, and research centers. With Nobel and Pulitzer writers on board, articles are created through the lens of various cultures, lifestyles, societies, and philanthropy. The articles on the Impakter platform cover critiques, positive journeys, current news surrounding sustainability, and the betterment of society in all parts of the world.
Coming from a place that often experienced the detrimental consequences of climate change despite contributing the least to the world’s ecological footprint, I appreciate the motives and actions taken by this company to shed light on pressing environmental issues. My hometown, Saipan, Northern Mariana Islands, has survived numerous typhoons and tropical depressions. The island experienced nonconsecutive years resulting in the island’s devastation with 200+ mph winds. These platforms are small steps to mediating the negative effects of climate change and other environmental issues.
