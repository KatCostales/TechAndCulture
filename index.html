Our Mission Statement

We want to create a unique shopping experience while remaining sustainable. We commit to sustainable practices and high quality clothes. Fashion production makes up 10% of humanity's carbon emissions, dries up water sources, and pollutes rivers and streams. What's more, 85% of all textiles go to the dump each year (UNECE, 2018), and washing some types of clothes sends significant amount of microplastics into the ocean. Our mission is to spread awareness while minmizing the harmful affects of the fast fasion industry by reinvesting, recycling, and repurposing materials.
